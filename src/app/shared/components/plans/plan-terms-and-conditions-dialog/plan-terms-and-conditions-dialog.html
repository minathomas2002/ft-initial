Localization Plan Guidelines

SEC follows the below process to evaluate ‘Localization Plan’ and invite suppliers to join the BENA Journey:

1

Request Submission

Choose the product and submit basic details..

2

Eligibility Review

SEC team checks eligibility and priority.

3

Technical Qualification

Register as supplier and qualify the product.

4

Plan Approval

SEC reviews and approves your localization plan.

5

Welcome to BENA

Get a Relationship Manager to support your journey.

Key instructions to fill the provided ‘Localization Plan’ template:

All information requested in the template are mandatory and should be filled to the best of your ability and knowledge.

Each tab of the document is provided with ‘Instructions’ on the top. Please read these carefully while filling the tab.

‘Cover Page’ and ‘Overview’ pages are for information only and should not be modified in any manner.

Localization Plan must be filled for each ‘product’ individually. However, multiple specifications of the same product can be covered within the same ‘Localization Plan’, with all specifications listed in the ‘2. Product & Plant Overview’ tab Second field.

The signature disclaimer is displayed at the end of the template, and must signed it before submitting the plan.

It’s important for SEC to know that the product you are targeting to localize is purchased directly by SEC, or it’s approved suppliers. Please provide this information carefully in the ‘Targeted Customer’ section of the ‘2. Product & Plant Overview’ tab page.
